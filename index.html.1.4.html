About us

Delivering digital delicacies since 2003.

We are connected by our love of colours, forms, shapes, games and technology.

We met each other at Inertia – the first international design portal based in Hungary, which was a great meeting point for graphic designers between 2000 and 2007.

We always strive to convert the message of a client perfectly into the digital concepts, games, illustrations and online communication we are creating with strict precision, but we are not the kind of crew that stops there. What we are really fond of is when our creations not just exhibit the provided information, but offers memorable visuals and a long lasting, involving experience to everyone. Our real domain is the online world, but we are always ready to venture into unconventional and groundbraking offline adventures as well.

So we are here to convey messages, involve and entertain. And for this we don't see this goal and business as a bloody battle. We have a daily contact with the best of our profession, or the "competition" as others might call them.

We believe in the continuous exchange of technical experience and we are confident that sharing knowledge will serve the interests of the whole profession and our customers in the long run.
